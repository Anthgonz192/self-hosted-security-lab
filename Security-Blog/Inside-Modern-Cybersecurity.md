### Generative AI: Good or Bad for Penetration Testing?

**Summary**

This article explores the growing role of generative artificial intelligence in penetration testing. While AI can significantly assist security professionals by automating tasks, generating scripts, and improving efficiency, it also introduces risks if used without proper human oversight.

The post examines both sides of the debate:
- How generative AI can accelerate reconnaissance and vulnerability discovery
- The potential dangers of over-reliance on AI in security testing
- Why human expertise remains critical when conducting penetration tests

The discussion highlights that generative AI should be viewed as a **tool to assist security professionals rather than replace them**.
